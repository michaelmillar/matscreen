Features

What separates MatScreen from generic materials screening tools.

✔

TRUST / VERIFY / DEFER

Every candidate gets a triage label. TRUST means act on the prediction. VERIFY means schedule a DFT calculation. DEFER means the model cannot reliably predict this material.

🎯

Calibrated Confidence

Isotonic regression maps raw ensemble disagreement to calibrated intervals. 90% confidence intervals contain the truth approximately 90% of the time.

⚠

OOD Detection

Mahalanobis distance and ensemble disagreement flag materials outside the training domain. The model refuses to guess rather than pretending confidence.

☀

Solar-First Vertical

Shockley-Queisser efficiency, element abundance, toxicity flags, and supply-chain risk. Purpose-built for solar cell absorber discovery.

📋

DFT Queue Export

Export VERIFY materials directly to your simulation workflow as CSV. No manual filtering. The tool tells you exactly which candidates need computational verification.

📊

Reliability Diagrams

Visual proof of calibration quality, broken down by chemistry family (oxides, chalcogenides, pnictides, halides). Miscalibration area quantifies honest coverage.

How It Works

From 230,000 materials to a triaged shortlist.

1

Set your target

Choose a band gap range and stability threshold for solar absorbers. MatScreen screens materials from Materials Project and JARVIS.

2

Predict with calibrated uncertainty

A 5-member XGBoost ensemble predicts band gap. Isotonic regression recalibrates the raw ensemble disagreement into honest confidence intervals.

3

Detect out-of-distribution materials

Mahalanobis distance in PCA-reduced feature space, combined with ensemble disagreement thresholds, flags materials the model cannot reliably predict.

4

Triage every candidate

TRUST (calibrated std below 0.10 eV, in-domain): act directly. VERIFY (0.10 to 0.25 eV): run DFT. DEFER (OOD or above 0.25 eV): do not act without simulation.

5

Rank by solar suitability

Multi-objective Pareto sorting across SQ efficiency, stability, confidence, and element abundance. Export VERIFY materials to your DFT queue.

TRUST

Calibrated uncertainty below 0.10 eV. Material is within the model's training domain. Proceed to synthesis.

VERIFY

Uncertainty 0.10 to 0.25 eV, or near the domain boundary. Worth a DFT calculation before acting.

DEFER

Outside the model's training domain, or uncertainty exceeds 0.25 eV. Do not act on this prediction alone.
